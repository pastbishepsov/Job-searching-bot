## Executive Summary

Freelancer.com with authenticated Playwright access yielded **19 qualifying leads** from 35 reviewed.
Key finding: budget figures are hidden behind a subscription paywall even when logged in
(require a "Plus" membership to see exact bid amounts on others' projects).
Descriptions are fully accessible and allow accurate scope/fit assessment.

**Hit rate**: 54% qualifying (vs 17.5% in previous Upwork/Google search cycle) — Freelancer.com
is significantly better for our team because clients describe what they want more precisely
and don't default to "WordPress" as the assumed platform.

Top 3 picks are exceptional matches. All 7 picks represent clean, deliverable static HTML/CSS/JS work.
